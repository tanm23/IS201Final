Ballroom Dance and Me

I have done Ballroom Dance for 10 years now and it has been an amazing part of my life. Dancing has blessed me with amazing friends, experiences, and life lessons. Being on a team has helped me learn how to cooperate with others much more effectively and prepared me for hard situations.


BYU has always been a dream for me to come here and be able to dance in college and now that is my reality!
